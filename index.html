Leaflet.SmoothMarkerBouncing plugin demo

Let's see how it looks like!

  1. Click any marker to make it bounce
  2. Click on the bouncing marker to stop
  3. Make bounce some markers together
  4. Click anywhere on the map to stop the bouncing of all markers

Different markers have different options of animation:

- normal marker with default options

- ball marker with big height of bouncing and strong collapsing

- star marker, not collapses when touches the ground and no allows other markers to bounce during it's own bouncing

Check this demo on mobile device!
